Digital Twin is an AI-powered application that creates a virtual version of yourself, capable of answering questions, making decisions, and simulating your behavior. The app learns from your past data, personality, and communication style to become your personal AI assistant.Features
AI-Powered Twin – Your digital twin learns from your conversations, notes, and decisions.
Ask Anything – Get instant responses based on your own personality.
Decision Simulation – Test how you might react in different scenarios.
Conversation History – Your twin remembers previous interactions.
Secure & Private – All data is stored locally or securely on the cloud.
